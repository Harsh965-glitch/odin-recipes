lasagna

Description

lasagna is a classic italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel. its the ultimate comfort food that the whole family will love.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of bechamel sauce
  • 2 cups of cheese mozzarella
  • salt and pepper for taste

steps

  • preheat oven 180c
  • cook lasagna noodles to the packages instructions
  • brown the beef in a pan then add sauce, simmer it for 10 minutes
  • layer the noodles, meat sauce, bechamel and the cheese in the baking dish
  • repeat the layers till all ingredients are used/li>
  • top the remaning cheese on the top then bake for 50 minutes
  • let it rest for 10 minutes then server
