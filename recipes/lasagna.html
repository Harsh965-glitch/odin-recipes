Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel. its the ultimate comfort food that the whole family will love.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of bechamel sauce
  • 2 cups of cheese mozzarella
  • salt and pepper for taste

steps

  1. preheat oven 180c
  2. cook lasagna noodles to the packages instructions
  3. brown the beef in a pan then add sauce, simmer it for 10 minutes
  4. layer the noodles, meat sauce, bechamel and the cheese in the baking dish
  5. repeat the layers till all ingredients are used/li>
  6. top the remaning cheese on the top then bake for 50 minutes
  7. let it rest for 10 minutes then server
